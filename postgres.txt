Create a database
    createdb -e -E 'UTF-8' -O {dbowner} -h{host} -U{user} {database namne}

Connect to a database
    psql -h{host} -U{username} {database}

Turn on/off pretty formatting
    \x on/off

List databases
    \list
    \l

Switch database
    \connect {databasename}
    \c {databasename}
